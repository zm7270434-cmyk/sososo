Live captions for everything you hear — and say.

sososo is a free, open-source desktop app that captures your system audio and microphone, streams both to Deepgram for live speaker-separated captions, and ends every session with an AI summary. No account. No backend. Your keys stay on your machine.

Features

Built to catch every word in the room.

Two sources, two speakers

System audio (what you hear) and your microphone (what you say) are captured in parallel and diarized into two clean channels — so the transcript always knows who was talking.

Interim words, finalized in place

Captions stream in as you speak and settle into their final form — no flicker, no lost lines.

How it works

Recording in three steps.

01

Install

Grab the installer for your OS below — free, no account or sign-up. Windows and Linux capture system audio with zero setup; macOS routes it through BlackHole.

02

Bring your keys

Paste a free Deepgram key in Settings (plus an OpenAI or Gemini key if you want summaries). They're stored in your OS keychain and never leave your machine.

03

Hit record

Press Ctrl+Alt+R from anywhere. Live captions float over your meeting; finishing a session writes the transcript and an AI summary to your library.

On Windows, sososo also auto-detects Zoom / Teams / Meet / Webex meetings and offers one-click recording.

Privacy

Your audio goes only where you send it.

  • Bring your own keys. Audio streams to Deepgram; transcripts go to OpenAI or Gemini only when you ask for a summary or translation.

  • Keys in the OS keychain — Windows Credential Manager, macOS Keychain, or the Linux Secret Service. Never in a config file.

  • No backend, no account, no telemetry. Sessions live in a local SQLite database on your disk.
